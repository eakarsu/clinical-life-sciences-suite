## Feature Optimization

Similar donor-project capabilities are normalized into common feature names:
- Clinical trial matching, patient matching, and eligibility tools become `Trial Matching`.
- Pharma trial design and protocol assistance become `Studies` and `Protocols`.
- Drug interaction, pharmacovigilance, and safety case features become `Drug Safety` and `Adverse Events`.
- Research literature and document drafting features become `Medical Writing` plus the first-class `Literature Intelligence` group.
- AIResearchLiteratureAgent is elevated into `Literature Review`, `Citation Tracker`, `Paper Summaries`, and `Evidence Tables`.
- Lab and research operations become `Labs & Specimens`.
